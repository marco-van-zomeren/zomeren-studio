The Branergy Method is a framework I developed to align brand strategy with the deeper patterns found in nature, astrology, and human psychology.

## Why methods matter

Most brand strategies are transactional. They answer *what* but not *why*. The Branergy Method goes deeper.

## The four dimensions

Every brand operates on four dimensions: Vision (fire), Structure (earth), Communication (air), and Emotion (water). Balance across these dimensions creates brands that resonate.

## How to apply it

Start by profiling your brand on each dimension. Then identify what's missing. That gap is where the design work lives.
