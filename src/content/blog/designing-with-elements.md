Every brand has an elemental energy. The challenge of a designer is to find it, name it, and translate it into visual language.

## Fire

Fire brands burn bright. They use contrast, red tones, and dynamic compositions. Think Nike, Red Bull.

## Earth

Earth brands feel solid and trustworthy. Muted greens, structured layouts, heavy typography.

## Air

Air brands breathe. They use whitespace, lightness, and clean lines.

## Water

Water brands flow. They rely on gradients, fluid shapes, and emotional storytelling.
